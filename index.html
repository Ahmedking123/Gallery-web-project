Beneath the Sea of Tranquility, hidden behind a crater shaped like a sleeping cat, lies the Moon’s secret library. Its shelves are carved from stardust and moonstone, and the books whisper in languages no Earthling has ever heard. Every Tuesday, a lunar fox named Elio tiptoes in to borrow a volume of forgotten dreams. He reads them aloud to the stars, who giggle and twinkle in delight. No one knows who built the library, but legend says it was the first astronaut who never returned—choosing instead to become the Moon’s librarian forever.
